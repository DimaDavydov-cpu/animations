Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fiverr?

Fiverr is a global online marketplace offering a wide range of digital services, from graphic design and writing to programming and marketing. It connects businesses and individuals with skilled freelancers who can help bring their projects to life.

How does Fiverr work?

Browse through millions of services, compare offerings, and choose the freelancer that best fits your needs. Once you place an order, the freelancer will deliver the work within the agreed timeframe. You can communicate throughout the process and request revisions if needed.

What services can I find on Fiverr?

Fiverr offers services across 500+ categories including Graphics & Design, Digital Marketing, Writing & Translation, Video & Animation, Music & Audio, Programming & Tech, Business, AI Services, and more. Whether you need a logo, website, marketing campaign, or custom software, you'll find experts ready to help.

How do I pay on Fiverr?

Fiverr accepts various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Fiverr Credits. Payment is held securely until you approve the delivered work. This ensures both buyers and sellers are protected throughout the transaction.

What is Fiverr Pro?

Fiverr Pro connects you with hand-vetted, top-tier freelancers who have been rigorously evaluated for their skills, experience, and professionalism. Pro services are ideal for businesses and individuals seeking premium quality work and exceptional service.

How long does it take to receive my order?

Delivery time varies depending on the service and package you choose. Each Gig clearly displays the delivery time, which can range from 24 hours to several weeks. You can also filter search results by delivery time to find services that match your timeline.
